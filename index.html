About

The Lighthouse squad was formed in 2016 under the most hellish conditions you could think of, Highlands High School. The gang adopted the name “Lighthouse” in 2018 from the Migos song BBO (Bad Bitches Only) Featuring 21 Savage. The Lighthouse squad ventured to the shores of Longboat Key Florida in the spring of 2019, only to return to dominate the 2019 Annual Dart Wars competition. Currently, we are all attending different colleges, but have sights to return to Florida in the summer of 2020 to fuck shit up. Looking for girl companions please contact us below.
